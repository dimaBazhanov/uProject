Who can use Personal Sharing?

Updated in October 30, 2020 by Administration

There are two types of users that able to use our system, ordinary users and companies.

Ordinary users can rent property from companies that are Personal Sharing partners. Person need to create user profile first to gain an accsess to all available property.

Companies can offer their property by posting advertisment on this site. Only autorized company account can post propositions. That demand will guarantee users safety.

To get autorized account you need to perform next few simple steps:

  1. Register simple user account.
  2. Use this link and fill the form - Company registration form.
  3. Wait between 1 to 5 working days, you will get a ressponce for your request on mail or on site(or both).
  4. If you got there, feel free to post some advertisments using this instruction.
